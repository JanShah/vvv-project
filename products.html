Valiant is an interesting name

What"s more surpising, is the origin of it. These excerpts from surnamedb should pique your interest.

Last name: Valiant

SDB Popularity ranking: 12768

Recorded in some twenty spellings, many quite rare and including Valera, Valero (Italian), Valere, Valeri (French), Valero (Spanish) and Valiant (English). It may also be a "Crusader" name. That was a name that was originally associated in someway with the famous Crusades to the Holy Land in the 12th century. Many soldiers and pilgrims returning from these Crusades gave their children names associated with the bible and early Christian martyrs Read more at surnamedb.com
