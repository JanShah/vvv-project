Last name: Valiant

SDB Popularity ranking: 12768

Recorded in some twenty spellings, many quite rare and including Valera, Valero (Italian), Valere, Valeri (French), Valero (Spanish) and Valiant (English). It may also be a "Crusader" name. That was a name that was originally associated in someway with the famous Crusades to the Holy Land in the 12th century.
